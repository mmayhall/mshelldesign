Type West Marketing Materials

Type West is a postgraduate certificate program, hosted by Letterform Archive and geared toward graphic designers looking to expand their typographic skills by learning the tools and techniques needed to create original typefaces.

animated commercial for Type West


These marketing materials — including an animated commercial, poster, tote bag, and bookmark — were created in an academic setting as promotional materials for Letterform Archive’s Type West program. The objective of these items was to increase enrollment and name recognition, and convey a professional yet playful attitude.
