Disney's Animals, Science, & Environment Illustrated Materials

Disney's Animals, Science and Environment team's mission is to lead Walt Disney Parks and Resorts to CARE for animals and the environment, CONNECT people to nature and CONSERVE our natural resources.

section of okapi infographic, featured on the Disney Parks Blog


Each of these items was created to share Disneyâ€™s conservation and animal stories, and to encourage guests and cast members to get further involved in conservation and nature.

Items include an infographic about okapis, published on the Disney Parks Blog; a pair of posters for Epcot's The Seas; a set of 82 cards intended to be distributed among cast members, informing them about animals found in Disney's Animal Kingdom; a pair of worksheets intended to be completed as part of an educational nature program at Wishing Star Park in Shanghai Disneyland, featuring illustrations of both Disney and native birds; and sets of worksheets for children relating to Captain Marvel and The Lion King.
