Gale, A Cengage Company

Gale partners with libraries around the world to provide helpful databases and resources to patrons. Their brand is informative and authoritative, while remaining accessible and friendly through clean, simple designs, and bright colors to pair with their classic dark blue.

video announcing Gale's new motivation, mission, and vision statements, featuring intro and outro animations of Gale's logo and a slot animation of Gale's Empower lockup

These marketing materials and UI elements were created for Gale to better match their updated website design, and to better represent Gale's values and goals.

These goals are achieved with the Gale logo in the blade-shaped tab at the top left; the assignment of specific colors for each database category; and the consistent use of font, image style, and gradient.

Using these new icons, Gale required an update to their one-sheet template, which libraries distribute to patrons to inform them of what Gale databases are offered by their library. This updated one-sheet highlights the products, the new user tools within the products, and a call-to-action in the bottom left.

Additional resources created include a cohesive icon set—with an illustrated icon for each databas—and banners designed to be used in-product to display some of what patrons can find inside.

Software used includes Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, & Adobe Illustrator.
