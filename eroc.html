End Rape on Campus Marketing Campaign,
"This is Not Consent"

End Rape on Campus is an organization that works to end campus sexual violence through direct support for survivors and their communities; prevention through education; and policy reform at the campus, local, state, and federal levels

"This is Not Consent" poster series


This marketing campaign, created in an academic setting and titled “This is Not Consent,” contains a poster series and brochure for End Rape on Campus.

The goal of this campaign was to draw attention to the fact that a person cannot consent while drunk. From a distance, this poster may appear to be an advertisement for a specific brand of alcohol, but a closer look shows the label designs that say “This is not consent.” The resulting shift in the viewer’s reaction to the poster hopefully causes them to remember this message the next time they see or consume in alcohol.
