The Game of Chess

This redesign of the classic game of chess, inspired by Russian constructivism, is intended to convey the mental intensity of chess through sharp geometry, high contrast, saturated color and intimidating angles.

The Game of Chess: How the Pieces Move Explained in 45 seconds


This redesign includes an instructional book on how to play chess, a motion graphic explainer animation on how each piece moves, and board game packaging for The Game of Chess.

This packaging design was selected to be featured in Auburn University’s 2019 Juried Student Design Show, and this motion graphic explainer animation was selected to be featured in Auburn University’s 2020 Juried Student Design Show—where it won second place for interactive design.
