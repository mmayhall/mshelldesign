Auburn Outdoors Field Guide Cookbook

The Auburn Outdoors Field Guide is a curated collection of resources to help students plan their next adventure, with helpful information to locate climbing, mountain biking, and hiking/backpacking locations close to Auburn. The Auburn Outdoors Field Guide Cookbook is a part of this collection.

Auburn Outdoors Field Guide Cookbook cover design


This cookbook was illustrated & designed as a part of the Auburn Outdoors Field Guide. These backcountry cooking recipes were compiled by students who cook while on Auburn Outdoors trips, where there is no access to conventional cooking methods.

This cookbook won second place for the 2019 NIRSA Creative Excellence Award for student publication.
