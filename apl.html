Auburn Public Library Logo & Marketing Campaign

The Auburn Public Library inspires people to explore new ideas, pursue their dreams, and change their lives. They embrace principles of equality, inclusivity, and diversity in their collections, programs, and services. This is a hypothetical redesign.

Instagram post, page, and story for the “Check it out” campaign


The goal of this logo redesign was to modernize the public library with a cleaner and less dated look. This is achieved through the bright colors, altered san serif type, and strong use of shape.

The “Check it out” marketing campaign presents classic books in a humorous way to promote the idea that the library has book for every interest. This campaign includes posters found throughout the library, posts for the library’s Instagram page, and an animation for their Instagram story.

Software used includes Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign, & Adobe Photoshop. Photography by Michelle Mayhall.
