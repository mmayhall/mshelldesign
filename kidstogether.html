Adobe Creative Jam Nickelodeon
#KidsTogether App

To help kids and families amid the coronavirus crisis, Nickelodeon launched #KidsTogether, a global, multi-platform prosocial initiative with tips for kids and families on how to stay healthy during the viral outbreak, as well as ideas for activities to do together at home.

#KidsTogether app prototype


In partnership with Adobe, Nickelodeon challenged student designers to design and prototype an iPad app for #KidsTogether that safely helps kids stay connected to their classmates, friends, and family; allows kids to express themselves; and encourages kids to learn about health and safety.

Created for this design competition, this app helps kids through this challenging time by providing a positive distraction, with educational elements. Containing features such as messaging, gaming, and health tips, this app is for kids to use without their parents’ assistance.

Each day, upon opening the app, a new health tip appears on the home screen, and previous health tips can be found on the “TIPS” page. Then kids can take their minds off of this viral outbreak by playing fun games featuring some familiar Nickelodeon themes. They can also stay connected through the chat feature, and safely add friends using each friend’s unique code.

This app design placed in the top 15 out of 145 entries.
